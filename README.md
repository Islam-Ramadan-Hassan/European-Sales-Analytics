## 📌 The Story

<div style="background: #f8fafc; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 16px; border-left: 6px solid #f59e0b;">
  <strong>🧩 The Problem</strong><br/>
  A European retailer sold <strong>€10.67M</strong> worth of products (2011–2014) but made only <strong>€283k profit</strong> – a razor‑thin <strong>2.65% margin</strong>.  
  Several countries (<strong>Portugal, Netherlands, Sweden</strong>) actually <strong>lost money</strong>. The main culprit? <strong>Aggressive discounts</strong> (up to 50%) that destroyed profit.
</div>

<br/>

<div style="background: #fef9e6; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 16px; border-left: 6px solid #10b981;">
  <strong>🇪🇬 The MENA Connection</strong><br/>
  Egypt and the broader MENA region share similar risks:
  <ul>
    <li>High customer expectation for discounts</li>
    <li>Logistical challenges – average shipping here was <strong>~4 days</strong></li>
    <li>Categories like <strong>Tables</strong> and <strong>Accessories</strong> turn negative easily</li>
  </ul>
  👉 <strong>What if we could predict which discounts will lose money in Cairo before we offer them?</strong>
</div>

<br/>

<div style="background: #e6f7f0; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 16px; border-left: 6px solid #3b82f6;">
  <strong>🚀 What This Project Does</strong><br/>
  ✅ Cleans & analyzes real European sales data<br/>
  ✅ Identifies unprofitable products / countries / discounts<br/>
  ✅ Translates findings into a <strong>MENA‑ready pricing & discount playbook</strong>
</div>

## 🌍 Actionable Recommendations for Egypt & MENA

| Challenge (from European data) | ✅ MENA Recommendation |
|--------------------------------|------------------------|
| ❌ Portugal, Netherlands lost money | Treat Portugal as a **proxy for Egypt** – start with small discount tests |
| ❌ Tables & Accessories negative profit | **Avoid discounts on Tables** in MENA – focus on volume |
| ❌ High discounts killed margins | Cap discounts at **20%** for the first 6 months |
| ❌ Some customers unprofitable | Target **Consumer segment** (most profitable in Europe) |
| ❌ Shipping cost = 4 days avg | Use **Economy shipping** (most used in Europe) |

> 📈 **Potential impact**: Applying these rules in Egypt could increase net margin from 2.65% → 8‑10% in the first year.
